Sizzler

Good in taste and atmosphere
I really enjoy eating food here. There are many options for me to choose and
all the options are tasted very good. The price of this restaurant is also acceptable.

Not impressed
I came to eat here twice now and it does not impress me at all.
The steak is quite expensive and the taste is not that good.

After You

Soooo Recommend
It is one of the best dessert cafes. Its dessert is really good, one of the MUST
order menu is honey toast and brownie. It is very delicious. Although the price is a little bit high, it is worth it to waste your time and sit there to eat!!

Not suggested
I cannot reject that its tastes are really good, but I waited 1 hour before I got
my queue and the price is also too high when compared to the amount of dessert they give. Due to that, I would say I am not suggesting anyone to go!
